KESHAV MEMORIAL INSTITUE OF TECHNOLGY

ABOUT US

Established in 2007, KMIT is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad, and is approved by the All

India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. KMIT strongly believes in encouraging learner autonomy which the

administration and Faculty facilitate by building a learner centric environment. Also, continuous efforts are made towards

modernising and upgrading its resources and teaching-learning processes in tandem with the latest technological trends.KMIT has the distinctive advantage of being located in the heart of the city i.e. Narayanguda. This allows an ease of

connectivity to every part of Hyderabad what with the metro station, bus stop situated very close to the college.
